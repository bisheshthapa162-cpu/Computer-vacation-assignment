One of the most special moments of Dashain is the blessing of Tika and Jamara, which holds deep cultural and spiritual meaning. Along with Tika, the golden Jamara, grown carefully from barley seeds for several days, is worn behind the ears as a symbol of new beginnings and fertility. The vibrant rituals of Tika and Jamara not only strengthen family bonds but also fill homes with a sense of devotion, respect, and warmth, making Dashain an unforgettable celebration of love and tradition.
